🔔 Enable Notifications

To make sure you never miss a request, please allow notifications for Telegram on your device:

  • iPhone / iPad: Go to Settings → Notifications → Telegram → Turn on Allow Notifications
  • Android: Long-press the Telegram app icon → App Info → Notifications → Enable all relevant alerts
  • Mac / Desktop: In Telegram, click Settings → Notifications → Make sure alerts are turned on for private messages

🔒 Is this safe?

Yes — when you tap “Start,” Telegram gives our bot permission to message you alerts. That’s it.

  • ✅ We don’t see your phone number or contacts
  • ✅ We don’t store personal info
  • ✅ You can stop alerts anytime by typing /stop
