🎤 Welcome to rQuestify!

Hey [Artist Name],

Thanks for being part of the rQuestify beta! This platform is designed to make it easier for your audience to request songs and support you in real time — right from their phones.

Here’s what we need to get you set up:

📌 Step 1: Connect to Telegram for Live Notifications

You’ll get an alert instantly whenever someone sends you a song request. To activate this:

  1. Click this link: https://t.me/rquestify_bot
  2. Tap the “Start” button to open the chat
  3. Send a quick message like “Hi!” — that lets our system connect to your Telegram ID

That’s it! You’ll now receive real-time alerts when someone submits a request at your show.

🔔 Enable Notifications:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings → Notifications → Telegram → Enable “Allow Notifications”
  • Android: Long-press Telegram icon → App Info → Notifications → Enable
  • Mac/Desktop: Telegram → Settings → Notifications → Turn on for Private Chats

🔒 Is this safe?

  • ✅ We do not see your phone number or contacts
  • ✅ You will not be added to any groups or spam lists
  • ✅ You can opt out at any time by typing /stop

🎶 Step 2: Send Us Your Song List

Please send your song list in one of the following formats:

  • ✅ Google Sheet (preferred)
  • ✅ Excel file (.xlsx or .xls)
  • ✅ Text file (with song titles on separate lines)

Your list should ideally include:

  • 🎵 Song Title
  • 🎤 Artist (if available)
  • 🎸 Genre (optional but helpful)

You can email your list to: info@sanibelsong.com

🧾 Step 3: Send Us Your Details

To personalize your page, please send:

  • Your name / stage name
  • Profile picture or logo (optional)
  • Payment handles (Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, etc.)
  • Any welcome message you want fans to see

✅ What Happens Next

Once we receive everything, we’ll create your personalized song request & tipping page and send you a preview link.

You’ll also get a custom QR code you can print or display at shows so fans can access your request page instantly.

Thanks for joining us — we’re excited to help you engage your fans and earn more with rQuestify!
